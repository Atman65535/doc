# Group Meeting
In this document, the basic task of a group meeting is declared and one process checklist is provided for better information exchanging.
Firstly, the group meeting takes the responsibility of information fusion and exchanging.
However, usual meeting always lack of tightness for the clutter schedules.
We should establish a clear structure for better efficiency in meeting.

## Main tasks of group meeting
- Key information exchange.
  Here we have to filter the information with priority and information entropy.
  Keep a high entropy meeting, discard those useless.

- Tasks allocation and acceptance
  The milestones should be accepted at meeting.
  Audit group should evaluate the quality of the work of members, and provide some feedbacks.
  The feedbacks should be acceptance -> new tasks, or rejection with improvement suggestions.

- Other functions
  While a group meeting achieve the two criterion above, it is success already.
  Then some additional properties could be added toward the meeting, such as team spirit enhancing...

## Check List for meeting.

Here we provide a practiable checklist for holding a meeting.
- [ ] Problem Collecting
  Here you should collect information from your team and summary the target of a meeting.
  The function of a meeting is determined before the meeting.

- [ ] Pre-thinking
  A meeting outline should be established after problem collecting.
  Containing:
    1. The title of meeting
    2. The target of meeting
    3. Tasks of meeting \(such as the problems to discussing, allocating the tasks... \)

  Then that outline should be delivered to all members, while everyone should consider the problems before meeting.

- [ ] Meeting recording
  This is quite easy if the outline is clear.
  Please fill the tasks of meeting with the discussing result on meeting.
  > [!TIP]
  > During the meeting, the response time of members should be rigorously restricted.
  > eg: 2 min for member, 4 min for meeting holder.

- [ ] Issuing
  After discussion toward the problems determined before meeting, here should be another QA session.
  Control the duration of this part.

- [ ] Quality Assessment
  After meeting, you should reflect on the quality of that meeting.
  Here are 2 basic criterion for you to referring:
  - All problem are closed. A solution isn't necessary, but the action next step matters.
  - Members are evaluated. The previous work of members are clearly reported and well evaluated. New tasks are allocated if necessary.

- [ ] Archive
  You should archive the meeting after assessing. Here are some basic contents, and the archive can build from the problem collection.
  - Name and time
  - Target
  - Problems
  - Result
  - Assessment
  - Summary
