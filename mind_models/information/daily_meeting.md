# Daily Meeting
Meetings are essential for information exchanging and cooperation.
However, most of meetings are full of tedious dialogues without clear in
formation.
To resist the uncertainty, we should figure out what the goal of meeting and establish the communication standard for efficient communication.

This document will declare the function of regular meetings and provide a meeting template for higher efficiency.

## Function of Meeting: Information Exchange
Every meeting should contain clear goals and members should focus on those goals during the meeting.

We divide one meeting into 3 stages regarding the function.

### 1. Before a meeting.
Considering that the main function of a meeting is to solve problems, the one who hold the meeting should summarize all issues that the team should solve on the meeting.

It is essential to present the issues with requirement and acceptance criterion.
Without the acceptance criterion, the issue never closes.

### 2. On Going
On the meeting, the whole team should focus on issues defined before.
"Solving problems" is the main stream of meeting, rather than update status of members.
The goal matters rather than the form.
